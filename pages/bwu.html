HousemateHQ

Bachelor Project with SiT

Housemate HQ is my and my project partner Solveig Ranøien Engen Bachelor project that aims to promote sneaky socialization within student collectives. We have created an application that initiates socialization and supports collaboration within Studentskipnader's collectives. The app aims to establish initial contact with those you have moved in with. By creating a profile, you will be able to personalize it, get friend suggestions and join/add activities you can do together. By subtly taking the initiative, the app will contribute to unity and social activities together without the pressure of social anxiety.

Accessibility Web Page

Accessibility, usability and ethics

For the course Accessibility, usability and ethics(IDG2012) I created a website that is ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications). It has TAB navigation, detailed descriptions/image text, screen reader options and follows the colors/font sizes/audio/video rules from WCAG 2.1. The yellow boxes are navigation highlights with TAB in use, it highlights where you are on the page and uses a screen reader to read any text.
